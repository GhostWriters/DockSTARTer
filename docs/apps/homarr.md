## Description

[Homarr](https://homarr.dev/) is a simple, yet powerful dashboard for your server.
Simplify the management of your server with Homarr - a sleek, modern dashboard that puts all of your apps and services at your fingertips. With Homarr, you can access and control everything in one convenient location.
